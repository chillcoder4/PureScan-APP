New Version Released

What's New in this release:

  • ✅ Firebase Cloud Sync: Sync your history & profile to the cloud securely.
  • ✅ Secure User Accounts: Restrict access to your private search history.
  • ✅ Login & Registration: Seamless registration and email-password authentication.
  • ✅ Cloud Profile Storage: Store age, goals, activity level & upload custom profile avatars.
  • ✅ Cross-Device Data Sync: Log in from any phone or PC to access your details instantly.
  • ✅ Improved Barcode Accuracy: Direct Open Food Facts database resolution for barcodes.
  • ✅ Better History Management: Scoped Firestore transactions for clearing history.
  • ✅ Improved Security: Firestore & Firebase Storage granular access rules.
  • ✅ Performance Improvements: Lazy loading modules and optimized bundle flows.
  • ✅ Bug Fixes: Resolved camera freezes and scanner downscaling errors.
